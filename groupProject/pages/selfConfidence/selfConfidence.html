Tips & Tricks to Improve Your Self Confidence

Here are some things that may help.

  1. Treat yourself- grooming and self care is important. It seems simple, but something as easy as styling your hair in a way you like can help.
  2. Identify negative aspects(or people) in your life- and avoid them.
  3. Take care of your body- its hard to feel good about yourself when you are not good to your body. Dieting, Sleep, Exercise, and Meditation are all things that can help yourself develop a healthy relationship to your self image.
  4. Live your life for yourself- stop comparaing youself to others. Disassoiate from istagram, stop comparing others lifes to your own. Everyone is different so every ones idea of beauty, success, and happiness is different.
